About Us

Who are we?

Thank you for visiting Seoule-made.com. This site is the culmination of the frustrations of a Korean who has lived and worked in Bangalore for 3 years and finally got tired of all the requests for Korean cosmetics from his friends everytime he went back home! :) 

All jokes aside, it is no secret that Korean skincare/cosmetics are the best in the world, but unfortunately it is quite difficult to acquire these products reliably and affordably in India. Often, the products are either fake or the mark up in price makes the products too expensive.

I'd like to change this completely and offer all Indians a solid and affordable bridge to these wonderful products. Because it doesn't matter who you are, everyone deserves to have great skin whether you're a star in a Korean drama or engineering student working tirelessly to get your degree!

How We Do It?

We have deals and agreements set up directly with manufacturers and representatives of many of the major cosmetics companies in Seoul and Busan (the two biggest cities in South Korea. In many cases you might even pay less!). Our products are shipped and stored in our warehouse in Bangalore. 

From there, we can ship to any major cities in India including all of the northeast states. Given our specific deals and network, I can assure you that you'll never pay more for our products than a Korean would in Korea!

We Need Your Help!

One of the biggest lessons I've learned working corporate jobs in India, USA, and Korea is that companies don't do enough to take action on customer and employee feedback. Our current inventory is a test trial and based off of what's popular in South Korea right now. However, this site is for all the machas and machis in India (Sorry I couldn't resist!).

We want your feedback and suggestions no matter how positive or negative they are! Is there a product you want that's not listed? Just ask! We will most likely be able to get it in a matter of time. Is there a particular product that didn't meet your expectations? Please let us know so that we can assist you better in the future!
